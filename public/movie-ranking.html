The best movies according to Luli

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Pride and prejudice

If you're a fan of classic romance and wit, it is a must-watch for its stunning visuals and timeless storytelling.

La La Land

If you love musicals and heartfelt stories, this is a captivating film that blends beautiful music, romance, and dazzling performances.

The Danish Girl

If you're drawn to powerful stories of identity and transformation, it is a beautifully crafted film that explores love, courage, and self-discovery.
